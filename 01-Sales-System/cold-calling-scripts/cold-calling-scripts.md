# Cold Calling Scripts

## Core Philosophy
- Sound confused / casual, not salesy
- "I'm not even sure if it makes sense for you" = disarms guard
- Lead with curiosity about THEIR problem, not your product
- The demo does the selling — your job is to get them to the demo

---

## Script 0: Voice AI — Services Businesses (Recommended Opener)

**Version A (if they have a website):**

> "Hey [Name], the reason I'm calling is because I was doing a business project about booking more appointments and having less people go to competitors. By your Google reviews I can see you're already doing great, so I'm not even sure if it would make sense for you — but do you sometimes miss calls or take longer to get back to texts?"

**They say YES:**
> "Yeah it's normal. I actually made a receptionist based on your business as a project — it would make sure everyone that's interested gets their questions answered and booked in immediately. It turned out pretty good which is why I decided to call you. Would something like that even help you?"

→ Handle objection / pitch now.

**They say NO (they always pick up / respond fast):**
> "That's awesome to hear. Doesn't that take a lot of time away from work if you're [personalized — e.g., 'under a sink']? Or do you just not get a lot of people calling?"

If they say they spend time:
> "Yeah sounds like it would take up hours during the week. Would it help if you had something like a receptionist to handle getting everyone their questions answered and booked in immediately?"

If they say they don't get calls:
> "I see, that makes sense. If I could make sure more people called and needed your services, would that help?"

→ Pitch Google Ads / lead gen add-on.

---

**Version B (if they DON'T have a website):**

> "Hey [Name], the reason I'm calling is I was looking for a website with your info. Is there a reason you don't have a website, or did I just miss it? [pause] By your Google reviews I can see you're already doing great, so I'm not even sure if it would make sense — but as part of a business project I actually made a website for you. It turned out pretty good and it would let people find your business on Google. If you like it, all I'm asking for is a testimonial and I'll help you set it up for free. Sound fair?"

→ Book a 5–10 minute call to show them the website.

## Script 1: Aged Leads / Re-engagement

> "Yeah, [Name]? Hey it's [Your Name]. I was looking into [company name] and I was wondering if you could help me out for a moment? [pause] I'm not even sure if it makes sense for us to talk yet — but we follow up with all aged leads that you never converted to increase your new sales, while you only pay if it generates revenue. Our board members asked me to reach out to see if you'd be opposed to boosting your sales using AI, completely result-based. Would you be opposed to that?"

---

## Script 2: CEO-to-CEO / Consultative

> "Is it [firstName]? [firstName], I'm sorry — this is [Your Name], I'm the CEO of [Company Name] and I don't normally make calls about this, but our advisory board told me about your company. We usually help [industry] companies generate more sales through AI and I'm just taking a few people and figured to call you directly to get a feel for the market. Do you have a sales team for your sales?"

**Discovery questions:**
- "Do you have a lot of leads that haven't turned into contracts?"
- "Do you have an idea why some of those leads aren't closing?"
- "Do you think your sales team is losing sales because of bad follow-up?"

**Pivot:**
> "One thing we've done for another [industry] company like yours is create a customized AI and add it to their sales process — it engages and follows up with leads on SMS to find interest and book appointments to drive more revenue. I'd be happy to show you my system for free. How about we set a meeting [time] and I'll do it personally?"

---

## Script 3: Name-Drop / Social Proof

> "Hey [firstName]. We work with a few other [industry] companies like [familiar name]. It's [Your Name] at [Company Name], have you heard our name tossed around? We add AI to the sales team to close more deals..."

---

## Script 4: Pattern Interrupt (Disarming)

> "Hi [firstName], we don't know each other — if I mention this is a cold call, would that ruin your day? I'm with an AI company that adds AI sales reps to the team to increase conversion rates. I talk to [their role] daily to help them improve their sales team by adding AI. But I have a feeling you're about to tell me your sales team is already the best?"

---

## Key Tonality Tips
- **Sound confused / casual** — not scripted or salesy
- Pause after questions — let silence do work
- "I'm not even sure if it makes sense" = pre-disqualification (lowers guard)
- Always tie problems to THEIR pain (lost jobs, time away from work, slow response)
- The goal of every call = get them to the demo, NOT to close immediately
