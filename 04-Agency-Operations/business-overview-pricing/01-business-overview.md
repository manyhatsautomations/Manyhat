# Manyhats Automations — Business Overview

## Core Headline / Hook
> "You're Losing $90,000+ Every Year Because You Text Back In 2 Hours, Not 2 Seconds"

Speed is the #1 value prop. The average business takes 2+ hours to respond to a lead. We respond in 2 seconds, 24/7.
