## ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)
- SMBs with 5-50 employees
- High inbound lead volume (calls/texts they can't keep up with)
- Currently losing revenue to slow response times
- Industries: HVAC, roofing, landscaping, construction, fitness, legal, automotive
- Owner-operated (decision maker is reachable)
- Tech-adjacent but not tech-native (they use tools but don't build them)
