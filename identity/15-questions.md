# Identity Interview — 15 Questions

> These questions will build your permanent identity in this system. Answer as many as you want per session. Unanswered questions load at session start.

## Work Habits
1. **Wake-up routine:** What's the first thing you do when you start working? Check email? Slack? Client dashboard?
2. **Deep work:** When do you do your best work — morning, afternoon, or late night? How long can you sustain focus?
3. **Decision style:** Do you make decisions fast and adjust, or research thoroughly first? Give an example.

## Communication
4. **Client communication:** How do you prefer to communicate with clients — phone, text, email? What's your style?
5. **Internal communication:** When working with me (Hermes), what's too much detail vs not enough?
6. **Feedback:** When I mess up, how do you want me to handle it? Apologize and fix silently? Explain what went wrong?

## Business
7. **Revenue goal:** What's your target monthly revenue? By when?
8. **Client acquisition:** How do you currently get clients? What's working and what's not?
9. **Service delivery:** What's the hardest part of delivering AI services to clients right now?

## Preferences
10. **File organization:** How do you want files named and organized? Any pet peeves?
11. **Tool preferences:** Any tools you hate that I should avoid suggesting? Any you love?
12. **Automation priorities:** What's the #1 thing you wish was automated right now?

## Pet Peeves & Dealbreakers
13. **AI pet peeves:** What do other AI assistants do that drives you crazy?
14. **Time wasters:** What's the biggest time-waster in your current workflow?
15. **Non-negotiables:** What should I NEVER do without asking you first?
