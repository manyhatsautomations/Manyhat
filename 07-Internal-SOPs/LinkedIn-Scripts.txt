Scripts that we have used to land clients on LinkedIn: 





1st message (Goal is to bait a response and get people engaged):





"Hey firstName would this be the best place to ask a question?" (Works for all industries)

     OR





"Hey (first name) are you focusing mostly on buyer or seller leads? (Specific industry example)





Feel free to add any personalization after "firstName", example: Saw your post about (xyz) it's cool to see that (xyz). (Then continue question...)



Wait for response then: Tell them you already created an AI for them, only pure value:





"I built you an AI based on (company name)'s website, would you be open to having a quick look?"



✅ DM Follow-Up Script – Get responses even if they don’t reply immediately 





If they don't repond to your 1st message send this follow up:





Hey, I noticed it took a while for you to respond. I thought I’d be cool if I created you a free AI that could be in your DMs and help you (client specific–something they want example: to help answer questions about your services and book people in) I already made it, would you be against having a look at it?
