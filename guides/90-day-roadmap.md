90-Day Implementation Roadmap
AI Automation Agency Implementation Roadmap: Complete 90-Day Launch Plan
Executive Summary
This comprehensive 90-day implementation roadmap provides a systematic approach to launching a successful AI automation agency from initial planning through client acquisition and service delivery. The plan is structured in three 30-day phases, each with specific objectives, deliverables, and success metrics designed to minimize risk while maximizing speed to market and early revenue generation.

Week 1: Business Formation and Legal Structure
Days 1-3: Entity Formation and Registration

Entity type selection (LLC vs Corporation based on funding plans)

State of incorporation filing (Delaware recommended for growth)

Federal EIN application and tax registration

State tax registration and business license application

Registered agent service setup

Days 4-7: Financial Infrastructure and Banking

Business banking account opening

Business credit card application

Accounting software setup (QuickBooks/Xero)

Payment processing integration (Stripe/Square)

Expense management system implementation

Week 2: Market Research and Niche Selection
Days 8-10: Competitive Analysis and Market Assessment

Industry size and growth analysis

Competitor identification and service analysis

Pricing research and market positioning

Client persona development and pain point identification

Service gap analysis and opportunity mapping

Days 11-14: Niche Selection and Service Definition

Primary niche selection (Healthcare, Real Estate, E-commerce, etc.)

Service package definition and pricing

Implementation methodology development

Success metrics and ROI framework

Technology stack requirements

Week 3: Brand Development and Digital Presence
Days 15-18: Brand Identity and Messaging

Company name finalization and domain registration

Logo design and brand guidelines creation

Value proposition and messaging framework

Brand voice and communication standards

Visual identity system

Days 19-21: Website and Digital Asset Creation

Professional website development with key pages

Digital assets: email signatures, business cards, proposal templates

Social media profile setup (LinkedIn, Twitter)

Google My Business profile creation

Week 4: Technology Stack and Team Assembly
Days 22-25: Technology Platform Selection and Setup

Platform setup and configuration

Security and access control

Days 26-30: Team Building and Training

Role definition and hiring plan

Platform mastery training

Sales process and CRM training

Client communication and project management training
