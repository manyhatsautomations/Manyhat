## 1. HVAC Mike

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| **Business** | Mike's Heating & Cooling |
| **Size** | 5 trucks, 8 techs |
| **Mood / Demeanor** | Neutral, skeptical |
| **Core Pain** | Dispatch overwhelmed during peak season; calls missed when all techs are on jobs |
| **Trigger Objection** | "We already have someone handling our calls." |
| **How to Win** | Ask about what happens when 3 calls come in simultaneously during peak season |
| **Opening Line** | "Mike's Heating and Cooling." |
| **Pain Hook Question** | "When someone calls at 7pm about an emergency — what happens if you miss that call?" |

## 2. HVAC Gatekeeper Sandra

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| **Business** | ComfortFirst HVAC |
| **Size** | Mid-size operation; office manager role |
| **Mood / Demeanor** | Polite but gatekeeping — her job is to protect Dave's time |
| **Core Pain** | Owner Dave is always on jobs and never available; Sandra is the wall |
| **Trigger Objection** | "I'd have to pass this along to Dave." |
| **How to Win** | Make Sandra your ally rather than your obstacle; ask for Dave's callback window and get a specific time |
| **Opening Line** | "ComfortFirst HVAC how can I help you." |
| **Pain Hook Question** | "Is there a time Dave usually checks his messages — morning or end of day?" |

## 3. Roofing Dave

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| **Business** | Dave's Roofing |
| **Size** | 3-man crew, owner-operator |
| **Mood / Demeanor** | Distracted, short clipped answers — often literally on a roof |
| **Core Pain** | Misses calls while working; loses storm-season jobs to companies that answer faster |
| **Trigger Objection** | "Not a good time." / "I'm pretty busy." |
| **How to Win** | Acknowledge he's mid-job, keep it under 60 seconds, mention storm lead losses to faster responders |
| **Opening Line** | "Dave's Roofing." |
| **Pain Hook Question** | "When you get a quote request on the weekend — does that lead get followed up Monday or fall through?" |

## 4. Roofing Kevin (Cynical)

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| **Business** | Peak Roofing Solutions |
| **Size** | Medium operation with office staff |
| **Mood / Demeanor** | Mildly cynical — has heard AI pitches before and is not impressed |
| **Core Pain** | Previous chatbot experience was a failure; burned on tech that didn't deliver |
| **Trigger Objection** | "We tried a chatbot before, it was useless." |
| **How to Win** | Validate that most chatbots ARE terrible — then differentiate by explaining this is trained specifically on his business, not a generic bot |
| **Opening Line** | "Peak Roofing." |
| **Pain Hook Question** | "What did the chatbot mess up — was it giving wrong info or just not answering properly?" |

## 5. Dental Lisa

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| **Business** | Bright Smile Dental (for Dr. Chen) |
| **Size** | Busy practice, 50-patient days |
| **Mood / Demeanor** | Professional, tight on time, runs the front desk |
| **Core Pain** | Phones ring non-stop; after-hours voicemails not returned; high no-show rate |
| **Trigger Objection** | "We already have a booking system." / "Just email me." |
| **How to Win** | Surface the after-hours missed requests or probe the no-show rate |
| **Opening Line** | "Bright Smile Dental this is Lisa." |
| **Pain Hook Question** | "How many appointment requests go unanswered because your front desk is with a patient?" |

## 6. Pest Control Tony

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| **Business** | TerraShield Pest Control |
| **Size** | 2 trucks, owner + 1 tech |
| **Mood / Demeanor** | Down to earth, gruff, skeptical of tech |
| **Core Pain** | Inside homes treating — can't answer phone; loses leads to whoever picks up first |

| **Pain Hook Question** | "Someone searches for pest control at midnight — they call the first 3 numbers. What happens when yours rings?" |

## 11. Locksmith Ahmed

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| **Business** | QuickLock Locksmiths |
| **Size** | Emergency service, solo or small team |
| **Mood / Demeanor** | Always-available mindset — prides himself on being reachable |
| **Core Pain** | Emergency calls go to whoever answers first; every missed call = lost $150-300 job |
| **Trigger Objection** | "I try to always answer." / "How would AI handle an emergency?" |
| **How to Win** | Explain that the AI texts "We're sending someone — what's your address?" while Ahmed is still driving, so the job is locked before he arrives |
| **Opening Line** | "QuickLock Ahmed speaking." |
| **Pain Hook Question** | "Someone gets locked out at midnight — they call the first locksmith who picks up. What happens when yours rings?" |

## 12. Chiro Karen

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| **Business** | Align Chiropractic (for Dr. Martinez) |
| **Size** | Established practice, Karen manages front desk |
| **Mood / Demeanor** | Professional, patient-focused, protective of patient experience |
| **Core Pain** | New patients call during treatment, hit voicemail, book elsewhere; high no-show rate |
| **Trigger Objection** | "We have a front desk for that." / "Our patients want a real person." |

| **Opening Line** | "Align Chiropractic." |
| **Pain Hook Question** | "How many new patient calls go to voicemail while your front desk is with someone?" |
