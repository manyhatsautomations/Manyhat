SECTION 2: PRICING MODELS

Turf Bros does not publish specific pricing online. All projects are custom-
quoted based on site evaluation. The AI should guide users toward a free
consultation.

General Pricing Philosophy:
- Custom Quotes: Every project is unique.
- Free Consultation & Estimate: Primary call-to-action.

Factors Influencing Price:
- Square Footage
- Turf Type (Pet, Play, Premium Landscape, etc.)
- Site Preparation (removal, grading, base layer)
- Additional Features (shock pads, custom contours)

Indicative Pricing Ranges (CAD per sq. ft. installed):
