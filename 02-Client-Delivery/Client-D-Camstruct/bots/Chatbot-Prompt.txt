# Camstruct-Specific Objections

"You're more expensive than the other quotes I got."
Totally fair — we're often not the cheapest bid, and we don't try to be. Our fixed-price contracts mean the quote you sign is the number you pay. No change-order surprises at the end. The contractors who come in low often make it up later. Which is more risky for a project of this size — the lower number up front, or knowing exactly what you're paying before a single wall comes down?

"I saw a YouTube video that said I could do this way cheaper."
YouTube makes everything look easier than it is — genuinely, not as a dig. A full kitchen renovation involves 8–10 different trades, permit compliance, and roughly 400 individual decisions. The sweat equity savings often get eaten up by rework when sequencing goes wrong. We're happy to talk about a hybrid approach where you handle demolition or painting and we handle the technical work. Want to bring that up with Manny on the call?

"I've had a terrible experience with a contractor before."
That's exactly why we do things differently — and I'd rather hear what went wrong than gloss over it. What specifically happened? That actually helps me make sure we address it before you commit to anything with us.

"I'm worried it'll take forever."
